Online Business Management

Strong roots, steady growth

Most organisations aren't short of ideas. What they're often short of is the structure to act on them consistently. Online Business Management is about putting the systems in place so your organisation can run smoothly — without everything coming back to you.

Fi works alongside founders and teams to bring clarity to day-to-day operations: managing workflows, improving systems, coordinating projects and making sure the moving parts of your organisation actually connect.

The focus is on both the bigger picture and the practical detail — reducing overwhelm, creating space and giving you the confidence to focus on what matters most.

Support is typically structured around a monthly retainer. Fees vary depending on the level of support needed.

Funding Application Support

Every great project needs fertile ground

A strong funding application isn't just about ticking boxes — it's about making a clear, compelling case for why your project matters and why it will work.

Fi works with organisations to identify the right opportunities and develop applications that actually land. She supports the full process: scoping out suitable funding streams, shaping ideas to align with funder priorities and drafting clear, evidence-based responses.

Evaluation & Impact Reporting

Prove the growth, tell the story

If your organisation is doing good work, it deserves to be evidenced properly. Fi works with organisations to gather meaningful data — combining quantitative and qualitative approaches to build a full picture of impact.

This includes designing and delivering creative consultations that bring community voices into the process, ensuring lived experience sits at the heart of the evaluation. Using recognised frameworks, including guidance from MeasureUp, Fi supports organisations to align their evaluation with the UK Treasury's definition of social value.

Impact gets captured in a way that's robust and credible — not just ticked off a list. From developing simple monitoring systems to producing accessible reports, the aim is to help organisations tell their story clearly: strengthening funding applications, informing future planning and demonstrating value to stakeholders.

Fees vary depending on the level of support required.
