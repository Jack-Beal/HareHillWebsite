Online Business Management

Strong roots, steady growth

Most organisations aren't short of ideas. What they're often short of is the structure to act on them consistently. Online Business Management is about putting the systems in place so your organisation can run smoothly, without everything coming back to you.

Fi works alongside founders and teams to bring clarity to day-to-day operations: managing workflows, improving systems, coordinating projects and making sure the moving parts of your organisation actually connect.

The focus is on both the bigger picture and the practical detail, freeing up space to do the work that matters.

Support is typically structured around a monthly retainer. Fees vary depending on the level of support needed.

Strategic Support

From the hilltop, everything is clearer

Strategy sessions work best when they're honest. That means creating real space for people to think, not just producing a document.

Fi facilitates structured workshops that draw out ideas, insights and priorities from across your organisation. She creates the conditions for open discussion, helping teams reflect on where they are, where they want to be and how they're going to get there. Every voice gets heard, and the output is a clear, actionable plan, not a report that sits in a folder.

The result is a shared sense of direction that people actually feel confident delivering.

Fees are typically structured on a day rate, varying depending on the level of support required.

Funding Application Support

Every great project needs fertile ground

A strong funding application needs to make a clear, compelling case for why your project matters and why it will work.

Fi works with organisations to identify the right opportunities and develop applications that actually land. She supports the full process: scoping out suitable funding streams, shaping ideas to align with funder priorities and drafting clear, evidence based responses.

Having secured funding and managed funded projects herself, she understands what funders are looking for and how to present a project well. Whether you need help at the idea stage, developing a proposal or completing an application from scratch, Fi brings clarity and structure to the process.

Fees vary depending on the level of support required.

Evaluation & Impact Reporting

Prove the growth, tell the story

If your organisation is doing good work, it deserves to be evidenced properly. Fi works with organisations to gather meaningful data, combining quantitative and qualitative approaches to build a full picture of impact.

This includes creative consultations that bring community voices directly into the process, so lived experience is part of the evaluation rather than an afterthought. Using recognised frameworks, including guidance from MeasureUp, Fi supports organisations to align their evaluation with the UK Treasury's definition of social value.

Impact gets captured in a way that's robust and credible, going beyond basic monitoring. From developing simple monitoring systems to producing accessible reports, the aim is to help organisations tell their story clearly: strengthening funding applications, informing future planning and demonstrating value to stakeholders.

Fees vary depending on the level of support required.

Social Media Content Creation

Nurture your presence

Social media should feel like your organisation, not like something bolted on. Fi offers two levels of support, designed to meet you where you are.

Seed: Consistent Presence

£300/month

For organisations looking to build or maintain a steady online presence. Includes regular content creation, caption writing, image design or selection and scheduling across your chosen platforms. A shared content calendar keeps things visible and aligned. Based on three posts per week.

Bloom: Community & Connection

£360/month

For organisations ready to deepen engagement. Everything in Seed, plus active platform management: responding to comments and messages, encouraging interaction and nurturing community. The aim is to build real engagement, not just grow a follower count. Based on three posts per week.

Event Systems & Promotion

Where good things gather

Fi supports organisations to set up and manage booking systems using tools like Eventbrite and Ticket Tailor, creating events, structuring ticket options and making sure the booking experience is smooth for participants.

Alongside that, she works with organisations to promote events through social media, building momentum and maximising reach. The aim is to take the pressure off the technical setup and promotion so teams can focus on delivering something good.

Fees vary depending on the level of support required.

Website Build & Maintain

Put down roots online

Your website should look like you and work for the people using it. Delivered in collaboration with associate Jack, this service covers the design and build of clean, considered websites: thoughtful layouts, clear content and a finished result that feels honest to your organisation.

Fi works closely with you on structure, messaging and direction. Jack leads the technical build and design. Beyond the initial build, ongoing support is available to keep content fresh, relevant and up to date.

Fees vary depending on the scope and complexity of the project.
